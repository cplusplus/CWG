1211. Misaligned lvalues

Section: 6.7.3  [basic.align]     Status: open     Submitter: David Svoboda     Date: 2010-10-20

6.7.3 [basic.align] speaks of “alignment requirements,” and 6.7.6.5.2 [basic.stc.dynamic.allocation] requires the result of an allocation function to point to “suitably aligned” storage, but there is no explicit statement of what happens when these requirements are violated (presumably undefined behavior).
